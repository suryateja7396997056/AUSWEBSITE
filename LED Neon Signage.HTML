The Modern Evolution of Illumination.

Classic glass neon has a nostalgic charm, but it is fragile, expensive to run, and contains hazardous gases. Budget Pro Solutions utilizes state-of-the-art LED Neon Flex technology to deliver that iconic glow without the drawbacks.

Our custom LED neon signs are meticulously handcrafted by molding flexible silicone tubes embedded with high-intensity LED lights onto premium acrylic backing. The result is a safe, durable, and breathtakingly bright sign.

Energy Efficient & Eco-Friendly

Consumes up to 80% less electricity than traditional glass neon, keeping your running costs incredibly low.

Shatterproof & Safe

Made from durable silicone and acrylic. No glass to break, no toxic mercury, and safe to touch (low heat emission).

Instagram Ready

Neon signs act as organic photo-ops, encouraging customers to share your brand on social media.
