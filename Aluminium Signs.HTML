Engineered to Defy The Elements.

What makes Aluminium signs so special? They are crafted using Aluminium Composite Material (ACM)—a highly engineered panel consisting of two thin aluminium sheets thermally bonded to a solid polyethylene core.

This "sandwich" construction results in a sign that is incredibly rigid yet surprisingly lightweight. Unlike solid steel, it will never rust. Unlike timber, it will never rot or warp in the sun. It remains perfectly flat, providing a flawless canvas for high-resolution printed graphics.

100% Weatherproof

Impervious to heavy rain, extreme UV exposure, and coastal salt spray. Built specifically for AU/NZ climates.

High Strength-to-Weight Ratio

Rigid enough to withstand high winds, yet light enough to be mounted securely to almost any facade or fence.

Guaranteed Finish

We wrap our ACM panels in premium outdoor-grade vinyl, topped with an anti-scratch, UV-protective overlaminate.

Limitless Applications

Because it can be cut to any shape and size, ACM is the foundation for almost all premium permanent signage.

Shopfront Fascias

The ultimate upgrade for your storefront. We create massive, seamless-looking fascia panels that span the entire width of your building to establish a dominant brand presence.

Factory & Warehouse Signs

Ensure trucks and clients can find your facility. ACM panels can be printed in massive scales, making them the standard for highly visible, long-lasting industrial park signage.

Directional & Wayfinding

Sleek, unbendable panels ideal for car parks, hospital campuses, and corporate parks. They maintain a perfectly flat, professional appearance year after year.

Site Hoardings

For long-term construction or development sites, Corflute may not last. ACM panels provide a secure, vandal-resistant, and premium-looking fence perimeter.

Custom Routed Shapes

Because it is a composite material, we can CNC route ACM into custom shapes, curved profiles, or even intricate 3D logos with absolute precision.

Complete Installation

Installing rigid aluminium panels requires structural expertise. Our team handles the heavy lifting, using concealed fixings and anti-theft mounting systems to ensure a flawless finish.
