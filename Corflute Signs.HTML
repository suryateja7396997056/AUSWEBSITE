The Anatomy of Corflute.

Corflute (also known as corrugated plastic or twin-wall plastic) consists of two flat sheets of polypropylene separated by a ribbed inner layer. This unique structure provides incredible rigidity while remaining almost weightless.

Because it is plastic, it is completely waterproof and will not deteriorate like cardboard or timber. At Budget Pro Solutions, we print directly onto the substrate using high-adhesion UV inks, ensuring your message won't scratch or fade in the harsh sun.
