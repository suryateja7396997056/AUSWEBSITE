Choosing the Right Substrate.

Ordering a banner isn't just about the size—it's about the environment. Choosing the wrong material can result in shredded signs or torn fences. Here is our expert guide on which material to choose for your project.

Heavy-Duty PVC Vinyl

The industry standard for brilliant, high-resolution color. Made from solid, non-permeable PVC material, these banners offer the highest print quality and 100% opacity.

  • Best For: Indoor events, flat walls, retail promotions, and short-term outdoor displays.
  • Visuals: Photographic print quality; colors pop vibrantly.
  • Wind Rating: Low. Acts like a sail; must be mounted against a solid surface in windy areas.

Wind-Permeable Mesh

Constructed with thousands of tiny holes, mesh banners allow wind and sound to pass right through them. This severely reduces the "sail effect" and prevents structural damage to fences.

  • Best For: Chain-link fences, construction hoardings, scaffolding, and high-wind outdoor areas.
  • Visuals: Slightly softer colors due to light passing through the micro-perforations.
  • Wind Rating: Exceptional. The only safe choice for exposed outdoor fencing.

Industrial-Grade Finishing Options

A banner is only as strong as its edges. We offer custom finishing to ensure your banner mounts perfectly and survives heavy weather.

Welded Hems & Eyelets

The edges are heat-welded (not just folded) to double the thickness. Nickel or brass eyelets are punched every 500mm for easy zip-tying.

Ropes & Tails

Heavy-duty nylon rope is stitched directly into the top and bottom hems, leaving 2-meter "tails" extending from the corners for tying between poles.

Rod Pockets

A sleeve is welded into the top and/or bottom edge, allowing a solid pole to slide through. Perfect for over-street banners or premium indoor hanging.

Kedar Edging

A thick, corded edging is stitched to the banner, allowing it to slide flawlessly into aluminium tracking rails (sail-track) for a drum-tight finish on trucks or walls.
