How to Get a Fast & Accurate Quote

To help us give you the most accurate pricing and turnaround time, please include the following details when you contact us.

1

Measurements & Specs

Please provide the rough width and height of the area you want to cover. Let us know if you need specific materials (e.g., Corflute, Vinyl, Aluminium).

2

Photos of the Space

Snap a quick photo of the wall, window, or vehicle on your phone and send it to our WhatsApp. This helps us check for installation obstacles.

3

Artwork & Logos

If you already have a design, email us the vector files (PDF, AI, or EPS). If you don't have a design, don't worry—our design team can create one for you!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical turnaround time?

Standard printing (like banners and stickers) typically takes 3-5 business days from artwork approval. Complex architectural signs, 3D lettering, or illuminated neon signs may take 2-3 weeks depending on fabrication requirements.

Do you offer installation services?

Yes! We provide professional, fully-insured installation services across Australia and New Zealand. From simple window decals to elevated building fascias requiring scissor lifts, our team handles it all safely and efficiently.

What file formats do you need for printing?

For the best print quality, we require vector artwork files. This includes .EPS, .AI, or high-resolution .PDF files. If you only have a JPEG or PNG, our design team can help redraw your logo for large-format printing.

Do you ship Australia & NZ wide?

Absolutely. If you don't require our installation services, we securely package and express-freight signs, banners, flags, and stickers directly to your doorstep anywhere in Australia and New Zealand.
