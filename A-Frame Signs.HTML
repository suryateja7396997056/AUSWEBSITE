Choose Your Flag Shape

Different wind conditions and design layouts require different flag shapes. We offer three premium profiles.

Teardrop Flags

The fabric is pulled tight over a curved fibreglass pole, creating a taut, teardrop shape. Excellent for high-wind areas as they do not flap aggressively at the edges, ensuring your logo is always perfectly readable.

Feather Flags

The classic swooper flag. With a straight bottom edge that flutters in the breeze, feather flags create maximum movement and kinetic energy. Ideal for car dealerships and retail sales.

Block / Rectangular Flags

Supported by a horizontal arm at the top, these flags remain fully open even with zero wind. They provide the largest rectangular printing area, perfect for extensive text or large corporate logos.

Single vs. Double-Sided

We offer exactly what your location requires to maximize visibility while staying on budget.

  • Single-Sided (Bleed-Through) Printed on one side, but the ink bleeds through the fabric so the image is visible in reverse on the back. Highly lightweight.
  • True Double-Sided Three layers of fabric (Print, Block-out layer, Print). Reads perfectly from both sides. Ideal for heavily text-based designs.

Versatile Hardware Bases

Built Tough For Harsh Winds.

Cheap flags snap and fade within weeks. Ours are engineered for endurance.

Dye-Sublimation Print

We don't just print *on* the fabric; we bake the ink *into* the fibers. This creates incredibly vibrant colors that resist UV fading and won't peel or crack.

Fibreglass Poles

Unlike cheap aluminium poles that bend permanently, our premium epoxy fibreglass poles flex with heavy wind gusts and immediately snap back to their original shape.

Ball-Bearing Spindles

Our premium bases feature heavy-duty ball-bearing spindles. This allows the flag to rotate 360-degrees smoothly with the wind, preventing the fabric from wrapping around the pole.
