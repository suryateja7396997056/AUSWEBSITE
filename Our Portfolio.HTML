Retail & Hospitality Sydney CBD

Boutique Cafe Complete Fit-Out

The Challenge: A high-end cafe needed a complete visual overhaul that respected their heritage architecture while maximizing street visibility.

The Solution: We deployed matte-finish Cut Vinyl window graphics to eliminate glare, a custom illuminated 3D Aluminium Fascia sign, and rustic wooden A-Frames for daily specials. Foot traffic increased by 34% within the first month.

Fleet Branding Auckland

Logistics Fleet Wrapping

The Challenge: A logistics company needed to wrap 15 delivery vans with absolute color consistency across different vehicle models without disrupting their delivery schedule.

The Solution: Using premium Cast Vinyl, our team worked after-hours to apply full wraps. The high-performance polymeric overlaminate ensures the branding will survive harsh road conditions and daily commercial washing.
